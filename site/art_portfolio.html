projects artwork
Rainbow Skull

I drew this cow skull with conte pencils, using different colors in the shadows to create the rainbow effect.

I'm a Giraffe

I created this giraffe by first painting the spots and general shadows with purple and yellow watercolor, then cross-hatching more detailed shading with an ink pen

Apple Compliments

This still life is drawn with colored pencil. I divided the paper down the middle, drawing the left side of the apples in natural color, and the right side in their color compliment.

Jake the Wonder Dog!

This drawing is an oil pastel of my dog, Jake. I used a scumbling technique to blend the colors, abstracting the shadow colors by adding purple.

Rainbow Dancer

This is a watercolor painting I made of a leaping dancer, painted in grayscale with clouds of color pouring from her hands and feet.

Triptic of Roses

This oil pastel drawing is of three close up shots of roses, drawn in slightly abstracted color schemes.

Rainbow whale watercolor

This watercolor painting blends the colors of the rainbow as they move across the body of a whale.

Horse

This horse drawing was done with an green ink pen. The organic shapes and line work are based on the muscular structure of the horse.

Tortoise

This tortoise drawing is a red, monochromatic oil pastel that uses the scumbling technique to blend colors in the shell, and smoother blending in his belly and face.
